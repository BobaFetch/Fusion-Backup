MdiSectLogo

If not completed, then do the the following:

	Remove all ESI trademark related images from the MdiSect.SideBar
	and MdiSect.TopBar.

	Add an Image Control to MdiSect.SideBar.  Name the Image Logo.
	Place it somewhere below the GroupButtons.

	Copy Logo and paste it on MdiSect.TopBar to create an IndexArray.
	Place it somewhere to the right of the buttons.

Add the provided ES2000.bmp to Logo(0) and Logo(1).  The actual location
of the Images is controled by EsiProj.bas depending on the workstation
monitor resolution.
